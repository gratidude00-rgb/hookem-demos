Our story

A taverna shaped by Crete

At Taverna Harakas, the kitchen is a love letter to Crete — to the herbs that grow wild on the hills, the olive oil pressed from our family's groves, the fish brought in by Hersonissos boats at dawn.

What you'll taste isn't a menu — it's a memory. Dishes our grandmothers cooked, served the way they served them, on tables under the bougainvillea, with raki on the house and time stretched out long.
